MANIKIN AND MINIKIN by Alfred Kreymborg

SHE.

Manikin!


HE.

Minikin?


SHE.

That fool of a servant has done it again.


HE.

[_laughingly_]

I should say, she's more than a fool.


SHE.

A meddlesome busybody----


HE.

A brittle-fingered noddy!


SHE.

Which way are you looking? What do you see?
